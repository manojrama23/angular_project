S-RCT
SAMSUNG RAN COMMISSIONING
TOOL

S-RCT is a Centralized, Site Commissioning and Integration tool for the Samsung Radio Access Network Elements for various Customers and Network Platforms. This automates all the stages in Commissioning and integration such as Pre-Migration, Migration, Post-Migration.

S-RCT improves the usability and efficiency of the network operation engineers in the overall commissioning process and provide Real time progress and Automated reporting of the commissioning process.
